MY HERO

"Meddling When You Don't Need To...Is The Essence Of Being A Hero." All Might

All Might from My Hero Academia is my personal hero because of his unwavering dedication to protecting others and inspiring hope. Despite his immense power, he always leads with compassion, believing that a true hero not only saves lives but lifts others up. His humility and determination, even in his weakest moments, show that true strength lies in one's heart. As a mentor, All Might teaches that anyone can be a hero, and his ability to inspire greatness in others is what makes him my hero.
